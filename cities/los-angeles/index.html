üìä Proposition 50 - Redistricting Map Measure

What Prop 50 Does:

Allows California to use legislative and congressional redistricting maps drawn by the independent Citizens Redistricting Commission, blocking potential gerrymandered maps.

If YES wins: Uses independent commission's fair maps for next decade

If NO wins: Could allow legislature-drawn maps (risk of partisan gerrymandering)

Progressive Consensus: ‚úÖ YES on Prop 50

Who supports YES:

  • California Democratic Party
  • Equality California (LGBTQ rights org)
  • EnviroVoters
  • Mercury News editorial board
  • Streetsblog California
  • Polling: 57% support (Emerson College, Oct 2025)

Why progressives support it: Protects against gerrymandering, ensures fair representation, prevents Trump-aligned interests from manipulating district maps.
