üå≥ Measure 26-260: Portland Parks Levy

What It Does:

Creates ongoing funding for Portland Parks & Recreation through property tax levy. Funds park maintenance, programming, and access to recreation facilities.

If YES wins: Stable funding for parks, prevents cuts to programs and maintenance

If NO wins: Parks funding continues to compete with other budget priorities, potential service cuts

Endorsements: ‚úÖ YES on Measure 26-260

Who supports YES:

  • League of Women Voters Portland
  • Willamette Week (alternative weekly)
  • Oregon League of Conservation Voters
  • Coalition of community groups & parks advocates

Why progressives support it: Parks are essential public infrastructure, especially for communities without private green space. Ensures equitable access to nature and recreation.
